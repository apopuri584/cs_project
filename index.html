Why This Project Matters

For our engineering design project, we were tasked with creating a hands-on solution, which led us to build an air quality sensor. My partner Anoushka and I (Angelica) were naturally drawn to this project because air quality is a global issue that affects people’s health and the environment. While we designed the sensor with South Bend, Indiana in mind, our research and monitoring focused on air quality across the entire United States. What started as a class assignment became an opportunity to explore how pollution varies nationwide and to better understand a problem that impacts communities everywhere.
